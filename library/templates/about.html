Library Collection


The Library has a collection of 38150+ books at present. These include books on all relevant subjects for teaching, reference and research in addition to fiction, literature and general interest books to take care of the leisure and recreational reading needs of users. The Library has also developed special collections such as Gandhian Studies, Hindi books, Children’s books, Matrubhasha collection, etc.

Online Information Resources are vital for academic and research institutes in today’s knowledge economy. The library provides its users access to more than 2580+ e-journals. Further, as a member to the E-Shodhsindhu Consortium, IITI academic community is getting access to more than 6110 e-Journals and some databases. In addition to this, the Library provide access to bibliographic databases such as SciFinder Scholar, Scopus   and MathSciNet, among others. The Reading Hall provides a peaceful environment conducive to study.
